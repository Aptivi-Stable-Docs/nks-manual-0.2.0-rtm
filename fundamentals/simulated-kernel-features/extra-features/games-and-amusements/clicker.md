# Clicker

<figure><img src="../../../../.gitbook/assets/image (1) (1).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

This game allows you to gain virtual money automatically, but as you unlock boxes, you gain more money more quickly during the session.

***

## <mark style="color:$primary;">Game Controls</mark>

Below controls are supported:

<table><thead><tr><th width="130">Keybinding</th><th>Description</th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td><code>Q</code></td><td>Unlocks the first box or gives you $10</td></tr><tr><td><code>W</code></td><td>Unlocks the second box or gives you $50</td></tr><tr><td><code>E</code></td><td>Unlocks the third box or gives you $100</td></tr><tr><td><code>A</code></td><td>Unlocks the fourth box or gives you $250</td></tr><tr><td><code>S</code></td><td>Unlocks the fifth box or gives you $500</td></tr><tr><td><code>D</code></td><td>Unlocks the sixth box or gives you $1000</td></tr><tr><td><code>Z</code></td><td>Unlocks the seventh box or gives you $2500</td></tr><tr><td><code>X</code></td><td>Unlocks the eighth box or gives you $5000</td></tr><tr><td><code>C</code></td><td>Unlocks the ninth box or gives you $10000</td></tr><tr><td><code>ESC</code></td><td>Exits the game</td></tr></tbody></table>
