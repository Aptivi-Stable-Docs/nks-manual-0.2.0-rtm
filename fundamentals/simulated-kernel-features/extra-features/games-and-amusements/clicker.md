# Clicker

<figure><img src="../../../../.gitbook/assets/image (1).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

This game allows you to gain virtual money automatically, but as you unlock boxes, you gain more money more quickly during the session.

### Controls

* `Q` either unlocks the first box or gives you $10
* `W` either unlocks the second box or gives you $50
* `E` either unlocks the third box or gives you $100
* `A` either unlocks the fourth box or gives you $250
* `S` either unlocks the fifth box or gives you $500
* `D` either unlocks the sixth box or gives you $1000
* `Z` either unlocks the seventh box or gives you $2500
* `X` either unlocks the eighth box or gives you $5000
* `C` either unlocks the ninth box or gives you $10000
* `ESC` to exit
