# Snaker

<figure><img src="../../../../.gitbook/assets/image (59).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

This type of game is common. This is a game in which you have to let the snake be oversized while avoiding the walls and your snake tail. The apple, represented by the plus sign, increases your snake mass by one unit.

***

## <mark style="color:$primary;">Game Controls</mark>

Below controls are supported:

<table><thead><tr><th width="130">Keybinding</th><th>Description</th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td><code>Arrow Up</code></td><td>Moves your snake up</td></tr><tr><td><code>Arrow Down</code></td><td>Moves your snake down</td></tr><tr><td><code>Arrow Left</code></td><td>Moves your snake left</td></tr><tr><td><code>Arrow Right</code></td><td>Moves your snake right</td></tr></tbody></table>
